RegWatch is a regulatory intelligence platform built by MNL Advocates LLP (MN Legal), a Nairobi-based legal tech law firm delivering AI-powered regulatory briefings, document management, and compliance chat to fintech and crypto startups, SMEs, and international organisations operating in Kenya. Every piece of content that reaches a client has been reviewed and approved by a lawyer.
